Our Car and Services



The Car

We use our immaculate Mercedes E280 classic limousine which is an 8 seater and so can carry up to 7 passengers. The exterior paintwork is flawless and always polished to perfection, and our interior is a plush soft leather for maximum comfort.



The Decorations

As part of our standard service we provide exterior ribbons in the colour of your choice. We also provide our own silk flower arrangements for the parcal shelf, door handles and interior handles (as seen in the gallery images). However, should you wish to provide your own flower arrangements that can be arranged.



Interior Comforts

As all part of service we provide a hamper in the car containing water bottles, tissues, wet wipes, antibacterial handgel, mints and a mirror. Should you wish to bring your own alcoholic beverage, we can provide glasses and a wine cooler upon request. We also provide a throw for should you feel cold and an umbrella should the weather turn, so that we can help escort you into your venue without you getting rained on.



The Chauffeurs

We have both male and female chauffeurs, all of whom will be immaculately dressed and well presented, they will be wearing face coverings throughout the duration of the pick-up and journey.
They will be there to ensure your pick-up, journey(s) on your special day go as seamless as possible and anything you need please feel free to ask them.



Hygene and Covid Safety

We are very conscious to make sure all of out customers feel safe when traveling with us on their special day. Therefore, we carry out a number of measures to ensure that high hygene standards are met. In between every journey the car inside and out is fully valeted and sanitised. The drivers wear face coverings and gloves throughout and furthermore we have a glass safety screen between the driver and passengers. Each driver will have also carried out a lateral flow test the morning before any drive, and in the event of this being positive, an alternative driver would be provided. We also provide hand sanitiser for your use in the car.



Areas Covered and Journeys Provided

We cover all around Gretna and the surrounding Dumfries and Galloway region. We offer from single journeys all the way through to multiple pick-up's and journeys. Please feel free to contact us with your requirements and we will see to it your needs are met.



Photographs

You are more than welcome to have photographs taken with the car and chauffeur. Please just inform us of when and where you would like the photographs to be taken eg at the pick up, ceremony drop off, reception drop off or even a stop enroute. We can then factor in any extra time needed to accomodate these photographs.
